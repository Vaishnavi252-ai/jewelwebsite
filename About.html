OUR STORY

Our inspiration comes from the jewellery makers of the Golden Horn, also known as Halic. The artisans of the Golden Horn during the Constantinople era were renowned for their exceptional skill and craftsmanship in the Byzantine era, a period known for producing exquisite jewellery. These artisans were celebrated for their ability to incorporate intricate designs and techniques into their work.
